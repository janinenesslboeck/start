About Me

Janine Neßlböck

Hello, my name is Janine Neßlböck and I teach Creative Dance for kids, Contemporary Dance, Show-Jazz and Ballet for children, teenagers and adults in Berlin. Ever since I can remember, the joy of movement and the love for music has driven me. In my lessons, participants are introduced to the fundamentals of dance through creative and anatomically adapted exercises. In a safe and inclusive learning environment, we will develop a sense of community in the classroom as well as the foundation for your individual passion. I look forward to see you!
